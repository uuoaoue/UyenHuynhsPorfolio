the new normal

2020
March 2020. The beginning of the pandemic. This project is to capture the time where individuals need to adapt the new way of living by wearing masks, practicing social distancing, ordering food as well as stocking supplies. This is a monochrome photography project decribes the daily life routine changing since the beginning of the pandemic. Going back to a year and a half ago, wearing mask when going outside was new to almost everyone. Now, we get used to it. Ordering takeouts spiked since restaurants are not allowed to host indoor dining. Everything closed. People started to stock food, water and especially toilet paper at home. Never can anyone can imagine that buying groceries can be that challenging.
