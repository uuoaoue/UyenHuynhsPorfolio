My Polaroid

for this project, I show a set of illustration that I drew over time that show my drawing style.
When I draw, I tend to choose pastel color for my design and tend to draw cartoonist style more than realistic one. In this project, I combine my illustrations with coding to draw a mouth that interacts with the mic. The mouth moves when you talk to the microphone. When you click on the polaroid, it moves to the left and shows the next photo. When you click on the background, it changes color. Every color in the project including the polaroid color, the flower, and the background is all random everytime you reset the canvas. Also, the flowers rotate relatively with the mouse movement.
