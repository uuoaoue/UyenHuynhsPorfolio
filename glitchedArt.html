Glitched me

2020
"Glitched me" is a project that I take a photo and use coding as well as Audacity to create the glitch to the photo of me being wrapped by the charging cores. I want to express how inescapable I am nowadays with all the technologies around me, and that make me glitched. The fun of doing this was I couldn't actually control the glitch because I was working with the code under the photo and some of them turned out quite interesting.
