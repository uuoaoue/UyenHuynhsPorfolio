My little world

2021
"My little world" is a group project, consisting of 4 members: Ian, Adrian, Lynn and myself. In this project, we decide to create a lego-concept where you can choose the pieces and put them together and create an alien world in everyway you want.
I used Illustrator to design the pieces such as the robots, sunflowers, flora, variety of hill pieces. Then, we used the laser cut to print out the pieces and connect them together.
