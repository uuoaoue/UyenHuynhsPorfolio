What's for dinner?

I am always hungry, I usually don’t know what to eat. Therefore, I created a randomizer to help me make up my mind. This is a set of the foods that I like to eat (mostly asian food). Whenever I hit “explore”, it will show my the food suggestion. Usually by the end of the randomizer, I can decide what I want for dinner. Sometimes, the food pops up in my mind ended up not displayed in the randomizer.

The main purpose of this project is to create a randomizer. Therefore, I used the stocked illustrations online to easily demonstrate the randomizing function.
