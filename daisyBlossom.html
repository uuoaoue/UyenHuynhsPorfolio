Daisy project

This is the project utilizing p5js to draw a daisy flower with the word "daisy" on the right side of the flower. When you hover the mouse into the word, it jiggles around the mouse. Also, the circle in the center of the flowers can change the color randomly by clicking it. The flowers in the background moves accordingly with the mouse movement as well.
