I'll quit Zoom

2021
"I'll quit Zoom" is the project utilizing Procreate and Adobe Premiere Pro to share my experience and thought about the impact of the pandemic on our daily life. The immediate shift to online studying during the COVID- 19 pandemic has a negative effect on self-perceptions, in my opinion. As an introverted, I feel comfortable staying at home, but I also want to socialize with other people sometimes and I think the virtual environment has its limitation. This project is a self-reflect for me about a time that has become an important period of our history and an opportunity to express my experience.
